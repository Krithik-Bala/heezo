The Philosophy

We believe in tools over articles, databases over opinions, and quality over quantity. If we can't make something genuinely best-in-class, we don't publish it.

Everything here is designed to make you feel smarter after using it. Not entertained for 30 seconds — actually smarter. That's the bar.

⚡ Built with AI

Heezo is powered by AI — from content research to tool development. But every piece is curated, fact-checked, and refined for quality. AI does the heavy lifting; human obsession sets the standard. We're transparent about this because we think it's the future, not a shortcut.
