But Marvel isn't one to give up easily, and after acquiring the rights to the characters, they've been working tirelessly to bring us a fresh take on the beloved superheroes.

With the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) expanding at an incredible rate, it's the perfect time to introduce the 'Fantastic Four' to a new generation of fans.
