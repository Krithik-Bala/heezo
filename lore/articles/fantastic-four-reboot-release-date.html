You guys, it's finally happening - the 'Fantastic Four' reboot is coming, and we've got the scoop! Marvel just dropped the news at Comic-Con 2026, and we can't wait to dive into the details. Watts has also hinted that he wants to explore the more sci-fi elements of the 'Fantastic Four' universe, which could lead to some mind-blowing visuals and action sequences. And, with a new cast on board, he'll have the freedom to create his own unique take on the characters, without being tied down to any existing storylines or continuity.
